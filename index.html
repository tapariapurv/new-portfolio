About Me

I am Purv Taparia, a 9-year old student from Class 4 residing in Mumbai, India. I have coded this portfolio to showcase my technical and creative abilities.

I am deeply interested in coding and writing. I am proficient in creative writing and can write descriptive paragraphs and essays. I am also quite fluent in English and possess a decent vocabulary and understanding of grammar concepts.

I have achieved International Rank 1 in the International English Olympiad conducted by the Science Olympiad Foundation twice. I also have created many projects on Scratch.

I also have many skills in fields such as Artificial Intelligence. I have used AI models such as Veo 3.1 to create production-class videos and models like Nano Banana Pro to create mesmerising images. I can also use tools from Microsoft Office with ease such as Excel, PowerPoint, Word, etc.

Along with this, I can also program in languages such as HTML and CSS and hold some knowledge about languages such as Python and C++. I also understand simple programming logic such as if, else and elif conditions and for and while loops. I also know about variables, lists, sets, tuples, dictionaries, functions, etc.
